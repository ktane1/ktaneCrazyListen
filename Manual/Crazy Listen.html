On the Subject of Crazy Listen

Now, the roles are reversed.

The module presents three play buttons and three submit buttons. Each play button corresponds to each submit button.

At first, the submit buttons display a number and has a colour of either blue or red. The play buttons also plays a voice clip of a speaker introducing themselves. This results in a total of three speakers behind each play button.

Recognize the voices of the speakers, then press either of the submit buttons. Now, pressing the play buttons should play a voice clip of a different quote. The speakers behind each play button will be shuffled, but will not be changed.

To solve the module, press the three submit buttons in a certain time in the correct order, based on the played voice clip.

A strike would reset the module back to the initial state, but the speakers will still stay the same.

Submission Order

Observe the table in the next page, which contains a list of speaker names, each assigned with a number.

For each submit button, take the speaker name behind the corresponding play button, and get its assigned number.

Take this number, and add the number displayed on the submit button if the button is blue. However, if the button is red, subtract the displayed number instead.

This is the final number for each submit button. Press the submit buttons corresponding to their final numbers in ascending order, priortizing the upmost button for ties.

Submission Time

Refer to Appendix FUNN1 for a list of quotes used by the module. Each quote also corresponds to a certain number.

The speaker, while speaking a quote, may also emphasize only one of the words in the quote. Note that speakers who speak other quotes will never emphasize any word.

Take the numerical position of this emphasized word in the quote, then multiply that with the speaker's assigned number. If none of the words are being emphasized, take the last digit of the serial number instead, replacing 0 with 7 if needed.

Observe this number and the quote's corresponding number. If none of the numbers share a digit, then press submit when he last digit of the timer display none of the digits in both numbers. Otherwise, press submit when the last digit of the seconds timer displays one of the shared digits.

*Due to technical reasons, their voice clips are actually a shiba inu barking, which always corresponds to other quotes in the table.
