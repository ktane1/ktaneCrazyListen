On the Subject of Crazy Listen

Now, the roles are reversed.

The module features three play buttons each with a corresponding submit button.

At first, the submit buttons each display a number and are coloured either red or blue. When pressed, the play buttons will each play a separate voice clip of a speaker introducing themself by name. Take note of the voices of the speakers as you will need to remember them later, then press any submit button to begin.

Now, pressing the play buttons will play new voice clips. You will still hear lines spoken by the same three speakers who introduced themselves, but the order may now be shuffled.

To solve the module, use the instructions below to determine both the order you must press the submit buttons in and what specific timing each must be pressed with.

A strike will reset the module back to the initial state but the three speakers will remain the same.

Submission Order

The tables on the next page contains a list of speaker names each with a unique assigned number.

For each submit button, determine the speaker behind the corresponding play button and take note of their assigned number on the table.

If the submit button is blue, add the number displayed on that submit button to the appropriate speaker number. If the button is red, subtract the displayed number instead. This is the final number for that submit button.

Determine the final number for each of the three submit buttons. Order the submit buttons by putting their final numbers in ascending numerical order. In the case of a tie, prioritise the submit button that is located higher on the module. This will be your final submission order.

Submission Time

Refer to Appendix FUNN1 for a list of quotes used by this module. Note that each quote also corresponds to a certain number.

In some voice clips, the speaker will emphasize one word in the quote in particular. If the quote belongs to [Other Quotes] in the table, no word will be emphasized.

Take the numerical position of this emphasized word in the quote, then multiply that with the sum of the speaker's assigned number and the absolute value of the final number of the corresponding submit button. If no word is emphasized, multiply the sum by the last digit of the serial number instead, replacing 0 with 7 if needed.

Take note of the digits in the resulting number as well as the digits in the quote’s corresponding number. If neither of these numbers share a digit, then press submit when no digits from either number are displayed on either seconds digit of the timer. If the two numbers do share a digit, press submit when the timer displays one of the shared digits on either seconds digit on the timer.

*Due to technical reasons, Rdzanu's voice clips are actually a shiba inu barking, which always corresponds to [Other Quotes] in the table.
